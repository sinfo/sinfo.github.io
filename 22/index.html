SINFO

SINFO Conf is one of the biggest Tech events in Portugal, and is heading to its 22nd Edition.

During a whole week, thousands of participants gather to see some of the brightest minds and greatest speakers in the world talk about some of the trendiest topics of today. Everyone will be able to try the newest gadgets and technologies in the market while being presented with an overview of the IT landscape by the country’s best companies. To top it all off, every day several workshops on the most requested topics will allow every participant to improve their technical and non-technical skills. Save the date, this is one of the biggest events of the year which you will not want to miss.
