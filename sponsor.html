Become a Partner!

See how you can help us create the biggest SINFO ever!


Description

Last year we continued with the improvement of our tent. Once again, all the different spaces and sections were joined in a huge tent in the middle of the campus and we felt like the disposition we arranged was better than the one in the previous edition. We had a record breaking attendance of participants - achievement we hope to repeat next year, from the 26th of February to the 2nd of March 2018. We invite you to visit our homepage presenting the several elements of our event. You can also visit the SINFO website, the 2017 edition, or see the video of last edition bellow.

Reasons to become a Partner

  • Do you want to recruit the best of the best from Portuguese universities? Are the more than 4000 participants, interested in Full Time, Part-time and internships jobs, music to your ears?
  • Do you want to promote, advertise and make your company known to future potential employees?
  • Do your want to network and form interesting business partnerships and connections?

Then your answer to all this is right here with SINFO! Check down below and be quick to book your presence!

Stand

You will have the possibility to rent a (or bring your own) stand during the event, at Instituto Superior Técnico.

Stand Specifications:

  • Dimensions: 3x3x2.5m;
  • Carpeted;
  • Back wall. (Side walls in case of being next to another stand);
  • Electricity - three (3) plugs;
  • Lights - three (3) spot lights;

  • Two (2) tables;
  • Two (2) chairs;
  • In the case of use of your own stand, the walls and lettering are removed.

Presentation		√√√x

Presentation

You will be able to make a company's presentation to an audience of students that will be at the event. The presentation allows the company to introduce and explain to the students their field of work, the projects they are or were working on, but also the job opportunities that might be available to the students in the company.

  • 45 min session divided as such:
    1. 5 min to present the company and get the students to learn a little more about the company itself;
    2. 30 min presentation (Preferably by Engineers or LEIC/IST Alumni);
    3. Semi-technical presentations tend to be preferred by the students;
    4. 10 min for Q&A.
  • Rooms with capacity for 50 or 70 students.

Keynote Sponsorship

Perks
  • Daily free-parking - The company will have a paid full-day parking for one vehicle.
  • Lunch discount - The Company members present during the event will have the possibility to have lunch for a very low price;
  • Parking during the presentations - The Company will have clearance to park one (1) car inside the Instituto Superior Técnico's parking lot for the duration of the presentation;

  • Access to the coffee-breaks - Access to the event’s coffee-breaks;
  • Wi-Fi Access - Free and unlimited access to Instituto Superior Técnico’s Wi-Fi network during the event using a company guest password;
  • Statistics - Statistical data and opinions of visitors regarding the participation of the company, gathered from surveys carried out, during the event.
