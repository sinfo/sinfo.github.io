Sponsor us!

See how you can help us creating the biggest SINFO ever!


Description

In case you are not familiar with us, we invite you to visit our homepage presenting the several elements of our event. You can also visit the XXI SINFO website, the 2014 edition.

Reasons to sponsor

  • Do you want to recruit the best of the best from Portuguese universities? Are the more than 4000 participants, interested in Full Time, Part-time and internships jobs, music to your ears?
  • Do you want to promote, advertise and make your company known to future potential employees?
  • Do you want to highlight your company's brand compared to its competitors?
  • Do you want to advertise your company nationally and internationally and increase your airtime on the media?
  • Do your want to network and form interesting business partnerships and connections?
  • Do you want to see the latest on the Portuguese IT market and worldwide tech hot topics, as well as the newest gadgets on the market and their applications?

Then your answer to all this is right here with SINFO! Check down below and be quick to book your presence!

Curricula

Every sponsor that acquires a sponsoring pack will be granted one (1) access to SINFO’s CV database, with CVs from students collected not only during the event but as of this year they are now collected, updated and processed throughout the year.

Stand and Stand ++

You will have the possibility to rent a (or bring your own) stand during the event, at Instituto Superior Técnico. Additionally, with “+University” pack this presence can be extended to more universities and more days, reaching an even higher number of students and potential candidates.

IST stand specifications:

  • Dimensions: 3x3x2.5m;
  • Carpeted;
  • Back wall. (Side walls in case of being next to another stand);
  • Electricity - three (3) plugs;
  • Lights - three (3) spot lights;
  • Lettering with the company’s name;
  • Two (2) tables;
  • Two (2) chairs;
  • In the case of use of your own stand, the walls and lettering are removed.

    Presentation    		1111*

    Presentation

    You will be able to make a company's presentation to an audience of students that will be at the event. The presentation allows the company to introduce and explain to the students their field of work, the projects they are or were working on, but also the job opportunities that might be available to the students in the company.

    • 45 min session divided as such:
      1. 5 min to present the company and get the students to learn a little more about the company itself;
      2. 30 min presentation (Preferably by Engineers or LEIC/IST Alumni);
      3. Semi-technical presentations tend to be preferred by the students;
      4. 10 min for Q&A.
    • Congress Center – rooms with capacity for 50 or 70 students.
